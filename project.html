My personal Projects

Statistics project

Research Topic: How does one's religious Affiliation affect the support for death penalty?. I used secondary data from Afrobarometer to analyse the support of death penalty for people who have commited crime from different religious group. The motive was to inform the government of Zimbabwe of the people's voice before they consider removing the death penalty policy. I used R and Excel to do the analysis.

Database project

A database to keep track of the current CAMFED beneficiaries and its Alumni community (CAMA members). A database system which allows external users (CAMFED Workers), to access the Identification details(name, date of birth, parents Left, type of scholarship etc.) of the current Beneficiaries, the CAMA District, the Core Mentor associated with that District, year of signing the scholarship contract, and year of termination. Also, the Database should be capable of changing the student from being a Beneficiary to a CAMA member after completing school. It should also be able to tell who went to which of the CAMFED partner University.
